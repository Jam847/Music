# Dark Divine

Formed in 2021 in Orlando, Florida, US, with current members:

* Anthony Martinez, Vocalist
* Robby Lynch, Guitarist
* Cory Piscitelli, Bassist
* Triston Blaize, Drummer

They are signed to Thriller Records.
